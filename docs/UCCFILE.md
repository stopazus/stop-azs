## 3. Collateral Description (Box 4)

> Example: "All equipment, inventory, accounts, deposit accounts, instruments, chattel paper, supporting obligations, and general intangibles of Debtor, whether now owned or hereafter acquired, together with all proceeds thereof."

## 6. Filing Instructions
1. Conduct a lien search to confirm name variations and prior filings.
2. Verify debtor name formatting consistent with the public-organic record (for entities) or legal name (for individuals).
3. Prepare the UCC1 form using the filing office's electronic portal or paper form.
4. Attach any required addendum forms for additional debtors or collateral.
5. Pay filing fees and retain the filing confirmation or acknowledgment copy.
6. Calendar continuation dates (six months prior to the five-year lapse date).

## 7. Exhibits and Supporting Documentation
- Executed security agreement.
- Power of attorney or authorization to file (if required).
- Collateral schedules, inventory lists, or account schedules.
- Certified organizational documents confirming debtor's legal name and jurisdiction.

## 8. Post-Filing Follow-Up
- Confirm acceptance by the filing office and correct indexing under the debtor's name.
- Provide filed copies to the secured party and debtor as required by agreement.
- Monitor for amendments, assignments, or terminations.
